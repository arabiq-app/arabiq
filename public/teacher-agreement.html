Platform Teacher Agreement

Please read this agreement carefully before signing. Your signature confirms full acceptance.

This agreement is between Arabiq Ltd ("Arabiq", "the Platform") and you, the teacher ("Teacher"). By signing below, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all terms set out in this document. This agreement governs your participation on the Arabiq platform and your relationship with students connected through it.

1

Platform Conduct & Professional Standards

As a Teacher on Arabiq, you agree to uphold the highest standards of professionalism, reliability, and respect in all interactions with students and with Arabiq staff.

  • Attend all confirmed sessions punctually and prepared
  • Provide notice of cancellation at least 24 hours in advance wherever possible
  • Communicate with students in a professional and respectful manner at all times
  • Maintain a suitable, quiet, and professional teaching environment for all video sessions
  • Ensure your device, internet connection, and video setup meet the required standard
⚠️ Repeated late cancellations, no-shows, or unprofessional conduct will result in immediate suspension or permanent removal from the platform without notice.
2

Non-Solicitation of Students

This clause is a condition of your participation on the Arabiq platform and is treated with the utmost seriousness.

You agree that you will not, at any time during or after your participation on Arabiq:

  • Directly solicit, contact, or approach any student whose connection originated through the Arabiq platform for the purpose of arranging lessons outside of Arabiq
  • Share personal contact details (phone numbers, personal email addresses, WhatsApp, social media) with students for the purpose of bypassing the platform
  • Accept payment from any Arabiq-connected student through any channel other than the Arabiq platform
  • Encourage or assist students in arranging lessons directly with you in any way
🚫 Any breach of this clause will result in immediate and permanent removal from Arabiq. Arabiq reserves the right to pursue legal action and seek financial damages equivalent to the value of lessons conducted outside the platform.
3

Non-Circumvention

All lessons arranged between you and a student who was first connected to you through Arabiq must be booked and processed exclusively through the Arabiq platform.

You agree that:

  • Any lesson whose student connection originated on Arabiq must be booked through arabiq.app
  • You will not accept alternative payment methods (bank transfer, cash, PayPal, etc.) from Arabiq-connected students
  • You will not create parallel booking arrangements outside of Arabiq for connected students
  • This obligation applies for a period of 24 months after your last session with a student on the platform
💡 This clause exists to protect both teachers and the platform. Arabiq invests significantly in student acquisition and teacher promotion. Fair use of the platform ensures it remains sustainable for everyone.
4

Platform Commission

You acknowledge and agree that Arabiq applies a platform commission to each lesson booked through the platform. The commission rate is communicated to you separately in your teacher onboarding confirmation.

  • Arabiq applies a 30% platform commission on all regular session payments. You receive 70% of each regular session booking directly to your connected payment account
  • Commission is deducted automatically from each booking payment before transfer
    • Trial sessions (£3, 30 minutes) are processed differently — 100% of the £3 trial session fee goes to Arabiq. Trial sessions are provided at a heavily discounted rate to lower the barrier for new students and are not subject to teacher earnings
  • Arabiq reserves the right to adjust the commission rate at any time with written notice. Any changes will take effect no earlier than 30 days after notice is given
5

Confidentiality

You agree to keep confidential all information relating to Arabiq's business operations, student data, commission structure, internal processes, and any other non-public information you become aware of through your participation on the platform.

  • Student personal data must never be shared with third parties
  • Arabiq's commercial terms must not be disclosed to competitors or third parties
  • Any content or materials shared by students during lessons must be treated as confidential
6

Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice. Arabiq reserves the right to terminate immediately and without notice in the event of:

  • Breach of the non-solicitation or non-circumvention clauses
  • Serious misconduct or unprofessional behaviour toward students
  • Repeated cancellations or persistent unreliability
  • Any action that brings Arabiq into disrepute

Upon termination, all obligations relating to confidentiality, non-solicitation, and non-circumvention remain in full effect.
